San José State University (commonly referred as SJSU) is a public university located in San Jose, California, USA. It is the founding school of the 23 campus California State University (CSU) system, and holds the distinction of being the oldest public institution of higher education on the West Coast of the United States.

Located in downtown San Jose, the SJSU main campus is situated on 154 acres (62 ha), or roughly 19 square blocks. SJSU's student population is one of the most ethnically diverse in the USA. Most of the San José State University alumni are hired by Silicon Valley firms than any other college or university and philanthropic support of SJSU is among the highest in the CSU system.
